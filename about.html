About Tech Layoff Watch

Tech Layoff Watch is a free, open dashboard that tracks layoffs across the global technology industry. Our mission is to provide transparent, factual information about workforce reductions in tech companies of all sizes.

What We Do

We aggregate publicly available data about tech industry layoffs and present it in an easy-to-understand format. Our dashboard includes:

  • Monthly trend charts showing layoff patterns over time
  • Company-by-company table with sorting and search capabilities
  • Running totals of affected employees
  • Industry breakdowns by company size and type
  • Timeline views of recent events

Our Data Sources

We curate data from multiple public sources including:

  • Company press releases and official announcements
  • SEC filings for publicly traded companies
  • Reputable news organizations (TechCrunch, Bloomberg, Reuters, etc.)
  • Industry tracking platforms like layoffs.fyi

Who This Is For

  • Tech workers monitoring industry stability
  • Job seekers assessing company health
  • Journalists covering tech industry trends
  • HR professionals and recruiters
  • Investors analyzing market conditions
  • General public interested in tech economics

Our Approach

We maintain a factual, neutral tone in all our reporting. Layoffs affect real people, and we treat this topic with the seriousness it deserves. We do not sensationalize or editorialize the data.

All data entries include source links so users can verify the information independently.
