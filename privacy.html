Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

Tech Layoff Watch ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Page visited and timestamp
  • Browser type and version (user agent)
  • Preferred language setting
  • Timezone

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or personal identifiers (unless you voluntarily contact us)
  • IP addresses (we do not log IP addresses)
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Financial or payment information

How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used solely to:

  • Display a visitor counter on the website
  • Understand general traffic patterns to improve the service
  • Detect and prevent abuse

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel - Website hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense - Advertising. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Adsterra - Advertising. Subject to Adsterra's Privacy Policy.
  • Chart.js - Loaded via CDN (jsDelivr). No data is sent to Chart.js.

Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to store your language preference and visitor count locally in your browser. Third-party advertising services may use cookies; please refer to their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

Visit log data is retained for a maximum of 90 days before being automatically deleted.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy
